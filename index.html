How Does Stereotypes Cause Barriers In Tech?

Human beings are society dependent organisms. We dependented but limited by society. As human beings, we often evaluate and held prejudice on everything we come in contact with. We deal with stereotypes in our daily lives, including those based on religion, gender, education, race, etc. Despite the fact that technology is now advanced and we lived in a time when 46.6 billions people using internet updating new information every moment, the formation of gender stereotypes is still an issue. Stereotypes are shortcuts for people to perceive and make categorization of the world in an efficient manner. However, if we stay ignorance and unaware, our perception and understanding the world will end up hurting others and ourselves.
